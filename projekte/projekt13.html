On découdre

The „Art, design et savoir–faire” Research and Creation Workshop, run by the Rennes site of the École Européenne Supérieure d'Art de Bretagne, offered a time of discovery and research around a material and know-how, and combines the three specialties offered by the school: art, communication and design.

During the first semester, students are invited to learn different textile skills through meetings with guest speakers. These initiations led to the development of personal projects that were exhibited for five days.
